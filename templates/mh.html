General Mental Health Information

During your period you may experience symptoms that negatively impact your mental health. Feelings of sadness, anxiety, loneliness,and irritability are all normal. You may suddenly feel ‘out of the ordinary’,overwhelmed or annoyed by everyone. It is important to take some space for yourself to avoid hurting others or yourself.

How To Manage

There are some simple ways to cope like meditating, watching your favourite shows or movies, and generally taking care of your body. Getting enough sleep at night, eating properly and exercising regularly can do wonders for your mental health.If you start to experience extreme symptoms of depression, panic attacks, or even suicidal thoughts it may be appropriate to talk to a trusted friend, adult or a professional counselor. You may need to be diagnosed with an illness like clinical depression. This may allow you to get the needed medication to cope.
